Common Applications of Motion Sensors in Homes:

Security: Motion sensors are commonly used as part of home security systems. When motion is detected in and around the home, the sensors can trigger alarms, send alerts to your smartphone, and activate security cameras.

Lighting Control:
Motion-activated lighting is an energy-efficient solution for both indoor and outdoor spaces. Lights automatically turn on when motion is detected and turn off when the area is unoccupied.

Home Automation:
Motion sensors can be integrated into your home automation system to trigger a variety of actions, such as adjusting the thermostat, turning on music or entertainment systems, and opening curtains or blinds.

Safety:
Motion sensors can be used to enhance safety by triggering alerts or turning on lights in case of emergencies or intruders.

Energy Savings:
Motion sensors can help conserve energy by ensuring that lights and appliances are only active when needed, reducing energy consumption.
